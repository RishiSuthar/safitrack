API Reference

The SafiTrack REST API gives you programmatic access to all your CRM data. All responses are JSON. Rate limit: 1,000 requests / minute per token.

Base URL: https://ndrkncirkekpqjjkasiy.supabase.co/rest/v1

Authentication

Pass your Bearer token in the Authorization header and your apikey header on every request.

curl https://...supabase.co/rest/v1/companies \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer USER_JWT" \
  -H "apikey: ANON_KEY"

Companies

GET/companiesList all companies
POST/companiesCreate a company
GET/companies/{id}Get a company
PATCH/companies/{id}Update a company
DELETE/companies/{id}Delete a company

Visits
