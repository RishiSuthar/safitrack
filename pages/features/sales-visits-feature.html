Verified Presence

Geofenced check-ins you can trust

When a rep checks into a visit, SafiTrack verifies they're within the client's geofence radius. Managers see verified badges on every visit — automated proof of presence, no manual GPS screenshots needed.

  • Automatic geofence validation on check-in
  • Configurable radius per client location
  • Duration tracking with check-in / check-out

AI Reports

AI-generated visit summaries

After each visit, Safi AI creates a structured summary of what was discussed, decisions made, and follow-up actions. Reps write quick notes — AI turns them into professional reports.

  • Auto-generated from visit notes
  • Key decisions and action items extracted
  • Photo evidence attached to visit record
