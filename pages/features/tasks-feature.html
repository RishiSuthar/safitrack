Connected Tasks

Tasks that live where your work lives

Every task links to a contact, company, or deal. When you open a deal, you see its tasks. When you complete a task, the activity logs automatically to the related record.

  • Link to contacts, companies, and opportunities
  • Auto-log completion to activity timeline
  • Create tasks from notes, visits, or calls
